Engineering Leader

I’m am experienced engineering leader in the fintech and software consulting space. I excel at building high-performing teams and delivering exceptional software quickly to surpass both stakeholder and customer expectations. A strong advocate for collaboration and fostering meaningful relationships, I work closely with cross-functional teams and stakeholders to co-create a vision of success and achieve it together.

I strive to cultivate an atmosphere of fun and collaboration within my teams, ensuring a harmonious work environment. One of my specialties is growing and scaling teams, ensuring they are well-equipped to face the ever-evolving challenges of the software industry. I am passionate about helping team members reach their full potential while delivering outstanding results.

Before entering the world of software engineering, I pursued chemistry and worked in scientific research. Over the past 8+ years, I've thrived as a full-time software engineer and manager. As a true polyglot, I have a knack for quickly adapting to new technologies and maximizing their potential.

I’m based out of Austin, TX. I’d love the opportunity to connect!
